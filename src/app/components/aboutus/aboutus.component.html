About Us

Ladiesworld012 is a women owned beauty establishment based in Ga-Rankuwa View , Pretoria and a new branch opened in brits . We are dedicated to providing high-quality beauty services including wig styling, custom installations, eyelashes, makeup, and skincare. Our mission is to create an inclusive and welcoming environment where every client feels valued and confident. We uphold professionalism, attention to detail, and exceptional service, ensuring that each visit is a memorable experience.
